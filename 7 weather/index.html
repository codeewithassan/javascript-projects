WeatherApp Usage Guide 🌤️

Welcome to WeatherApp! Here is how you can easily use this application:

  • 1. Automatic Dashboard: When the page loads, the table automatically populates with the current weather data for major cities of Pakistan.
  • 2. Custom Location Search: Enter the exact Latitude and Longitude of any place in the input fields on the top right, and click Search to fetch its specific weather.
  • 3. Dynamic Dropdown: Hover or click on "Get Your Country's weather" to quickly select pre-configured regions.

Note: The time shown in the dashboard reflects your local system's exact current time when the data was fetched.
